# **App Name**: Lex Magna Landing

## Core Features:

- Hero Section: Display headline, trust indicators, and attorney image.
- Service Listings: Display law firm's legal service offerings.
- Team Profiles: Display profiles of the attorneys working at Lex Magna.
- Testimonials: Display quotes of positive feedback from previous clients.
- Contact Form: Form that allows potential clients to submit contact information and request a consultation.
- Case Studies: Showcase a sample of past successes.

## Style Guidelines:

- Primary color: Deep navy blue (#0F1C3F) for a professional and sophisticated feel.
- Background color: A slightly lighter, desaturated navy, approximately (#1A2A4F).
- Accent color: Muted gold (#C9A961) for highlights and call-to-action buttons.
- Headline font: 'Playfair', a serif, for headings.
- Body font: 'Inter', a sans-serif, for body text.
- Scales of justice icon.
- Dramatic hero section with headline, trust indicators, and professional attorney image.
